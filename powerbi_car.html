Malaysia Car Insights Dashboard

Introduction

This project features an interactive Malaysia Car Market Dashboard built using Power BI. Data was sourced from data.gov.my, and car pricing information was automatically scraped using ChatGPT. The dashboard allows users to explore car pricing, model availability, colour distribution, and market trends across major car makers in Malaysia.

Project Overview

This dashboard consolidates multiple automotive datasets to present a clear, real-time view of the Malaysian car market. By filtering according to maker, model, and year, users can discover trends in pricing, availability, and consumer preferences. Makers covered include Proton, Perodua, Toyota, Honda, and BYD.

Key Insights from the Dashboard

• Minimum Price by Car Model

The bar chart highlights the lowest available prices for each car model, showcasing a price range from affordable units around RM35K to premium models exceeding RM180K.

• Colour Distribution Across Models

A stacked bar visual shows popular colour choices in Malaysia, with grey, silver, and white dominating the market. This helps understand production and consumer colour preferences.

• Car Availability by Year & Quarter

The line chart displays year-by-year trends (2020–2024). Perodua leads consistently in volume, while Proton and Toyota display notable seasonal fluctuations.

• Car Variant and Price Comparison

An interactive table allows viewers to compare pricing across car variants, trim levels, and years, making it easier to assess budget and value differences.

Skills Demonstrated

• Data Engineering & Cleaning

Processed and merged datasets from data.gov.my and scraped price data using automated ChatGPT instructions. Conducted cleaning, transformation, and standardization in Power Query.

• Data Modelling

Built data relationships, applied star-schema modelling, and developed DAX measures for interactive filtering and dynamic visuals.

• Data Visualization & Dashboard Design

Designed a user-friendly layout with colour-coded insights, slicers, and intuitive navigation to make analytical findings easy to understand for both technical and non-technical users.

Project Purpose & Value

This dashboard simplifies exploring the Malaysian automotive market for customers, analysts, and businesses. It demonstrates my ability to turn raw data into actionable insights, create interactive visual experiences, and integrate multiple datasets into a single analytical product.
