Sales Performance Dashboard

Introduction

This Sales Performance Dashboard was developed using Power BI to provide a comprehensive view of business performance across sales, profitability, customer behaviour, and product trends. The dashboard is designed to help stakeholders quickly identify key performance metrics, uncover insights, and support data-driven decision-making.

Project Overview

The dashboard consolidates transactional sales data and presents it in an intuitive and interactive format. Users can filter by quarter or year to analyse sales trends, customer performance, and product profitability over time. The visuals are organized to show high-level KPIs while also allowing deeper exploration through detailed charts and geographic insights.

Key Insights from the Dashboard

• Overall Business Performance

The KPI cards highlight total sales, total profit, quantity sold, transaction count, and profit margin. These indicators provide an immediate snapshot of the business's financial health and operational efficiency.

• Sales Distribution by County

A donut chart visualises sales contributions from various counties, helping identify strong markets and geographic opportunities. Los Angeles County and Orange County contribute the largest portion of total sales.

• Profit Trend Over Time

A line chart displays total profit by date, showing seasonal patterns and fluctuations from 2018 to 2020. Users can observe periods of higher profitability and identify long-term trends or anomalies.

• Customer Performance Ranking

A horizontal bar chart ranks customers based on total sales. This highlights top revenue-generating customers and supports strategic relationship management and targeted sales initiatives.

• Product-Level Sales Insights

A product ranking chart reveals which products are performing best, helping stakeholders make decisions on inventory planning, product focus, and marketing strategies.

• Geographic Profit Mapping

A map visual plots profit data by latitude and longitude, allowing businesses to assess geographic profit distribution and identify high-value regions. This supports territory planning and resource allocation.

Skills Demonstrated

• Data Cleaning & Transformation

Used Power Query to clean raw sales data, handle missing values, standardize fields, and prepare the dataset for modelling and visualization.

• Data Modelling

Built a structured data model with proper relationships and DAX measures, enabling accurate KPI calculations and dynamic filtering across time periods.

• Interactive Dashboard Design

Created an intuitive layout with KPI cards, customer and product breakdowns, time-series analytics, and geographic visuals to give users a 360° view of business performance.

Project Purpose & Impact

This dashboard enhances strategic decision-making by enabling stakeholders to monitor performance, identify top customers and products, analyse profit trends, and detect geographic sales patterns. It demonstrates my ability to convert complex datasets into actionable insights through clear visual storytelling.
